Pokémon Red

Start your Pokémon journey in Kanto with the iconic Generation 1 starters consisting of Charmander, Squirtle, & Bulbasaur. This version was released internationally.

Pokémon Green

Start your Pokémon journey in Kanto with the iconic Generation 1 starters consisting of Charmander, Squirtle, & Bulbasaur. This version was only released in Japan.

Pokémon Blue

Start your Pokémon journey in Kanto with the iconic Generation 1 starters consisting of Charmander, Squirtle, & Bulbasaur. This version was released internationally.

Pokémon Yellow

Start your Pokémon journey in Kanto but instead of the regular starters of Charmander, Squirtle, & Bulbasaur, you get a Pikachu as your partner. This version was released internationally.

Pokémon Gold

Our Pokémon journey continues on in generation 2 with an all new selection of Pokémon, starters, and even some legendaries in Johto.

Pokémon Silver

Our Pokémon journey continues on in generation 2 with an all new selection of Pokémon, starters, and even some legendaries in Johto.

Pokémon Crystal

Our Pokémon journey continues on in generation 2 with an all new selection of Pokémon, starters, and even some legendaries in Johto. This version includes some new features such as access to Kanto, some bug fixes, & the ability to play as a girl.
